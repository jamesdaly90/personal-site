James Daly

Self-taught Australian artist working in oil paints. Contemporary portraits and figurative works that celebrate the expressive potential of the medium itself.

Paintings move between raw gestural mark-making and precise, emotive realism. Faces emerge through bold marks and colour decisions, with areas intentionally unfinished — leaving room for the viewer to complete the image.

Based in Sydney, exhibiting internationally.
